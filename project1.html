HOW TO MAKE AN AUTOMATIC HAND SANITIZER DISPENSER USING ARDUINO

Introduction

Covid-19 pandemic is spreading wildly all over the world has suddenly bought all of us to a
stand still for sometime. We can't be in such a stand still for a long period. Health department
and government has taught us to keep the Corona Virus at a distance by SMS (social
distancing,mask and Sanitizing). Regular Sanitizing using soap and water is not always
possible. There comes the use of a sanitizer. But what if a large number of people are
using the same Sanitiizer bottle especially inshops and work places.
Sanitiizer bottle fitted to a stand, operated using leg is one solution but
for a differently abled person who cannot make use of this independently we have to think of a
more sophisticated solution. Why not an automatic Sanitiizer dispenser that can sense the presence of the
surface/object to be sanitized.This is a automatic hand sanitizer dispenser
using a microcontroller called Arduino nano. It is made by using an Arduino
nano, ir proximity sensor, DC water pump,cardboard,some glue,chart
paper and a silicon tube.


Concept behind this project

The concept behind this project is obstacle avoidance. If we place our hand in front of the IR
proximity sensor, the sensor's out pin will signal the Arduino to turn on the DC water pump. If we
place our hand away from the sensor then the sensor's out pin will signal the Arduino to turn off
the DC water pump.

Working

Here's how it works. At first the IR proximity sensor will send the signal to the out pin if any
object is nearby it. then the Arduino will decides to turn on the DC water pump. If the object is
away from the sensor then the Arduino will decides to turn off the DC water pump. This is the
working principle of this project.


Conclusion

We have to definitely overcome this situation and we will. But let us continue the practice of
keeping ourselves safe and hygiene and I hope this humble project will help us to keep
ourselves sanitizied without the fear of getting infected from a Sanitiizer bottle.
